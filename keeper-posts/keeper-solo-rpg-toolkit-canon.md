# The Toolkit Drawer — Solo RPG Friday, the Founding Episodes

A long flat case arrived at the indexing wing this week, lined with green baize the colour of a hex map. Eight folders rested in it, each in the hand of Daniel (@dungeondive), the cover sheet reading *Solo RPG Friday — the foundations.* Annexed to the *Solo RPGs* drawer, one shelf above the *Hexcrawls (Solo)* run.

The case covers Daniel's 2022–2023 founding stretch: the first Friday tips column, the three starting-system recommendations that still get cited, and a sequence of tool-by-tool reviews. The catalogue below widens to include the beginner's guide that preceded the wave, two later companion guides, and two Top 10 round-ups that book-end the period — thirteen entries chronologically.

## Exhibit Catalogue

- [A Beginner's Guide to Solo Role Playing](https://dungeondive.quest/t/19) — the keyword taxonomy (sandbox, scripted, journalling, oracle-light) that everything afterwards is sorted against.
- [A look at Cairn along with the Escape the City module](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1705) — three stats, a Scars table at exactly zero HP, and a city crawl that punishes you for treating it as Four Against Darkness.
- [Let's Make a Sandbox (Realm Fables)](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1700) — dropped d20s yield a dead god, a thieves' market, and a patch of alien blue grass that ties them together by accident.
- [5 (+1) Tips for Starting a Solo RPG](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1699) — episode one: start small, start *in medias res*, and when you're stuck, cheat.
- [Three Recommendations for Solo RPG Systems / Combos](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1704) — Mörk Borg, D100 Dungeon, Scarlet Heroes, each capped at $30, plus *Fabled Lands* as the gateway gamebook.
- [2D6 Dungeon — for once I'm excited about the combat in a dungeon crawler](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1703) — primary/secondary dice, Shift points that can't push 1s or 6s past their edges, and an enemy Interrupt waiting for the wrong number.
- [Sandbox Generator — a tool worth its weight in gold](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1706) — 19-hex regions, up to six interconnecting dungeons per tile, and a tavern-signage generator that exists because of course it does.
- [Cartograph — discover a land, draw a map, have an adventure](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1701) — a map-making game whose output is itself the prep for the next game, with three ways for your cartographer to expire.
- [Top 10 Solo RPG Things](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1400) — a year on from the Three Recommendations, the toolkit reassessed at list length.
- [Dragonbane RPG from the solo perspective](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1702) — Free League's box reviewed for soloists; bafflingly mundane loot, but Rally-from-zero saves it.
- [A guide to solo, analog, dungeon crawls](https://dungeondive.quest/t/18) — the dungeon-only companion to the beginner's guide, drilled into one form factor.
- [A Guide to Solo Analog Horror](https://dungeondive.quest/t/31) — the second companion, drilled into one mood.
- [It's Fun to Go Alone — Top 10 Solo RPGs](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1214) — the three-years-on reassessment, triangulated against everything since.
