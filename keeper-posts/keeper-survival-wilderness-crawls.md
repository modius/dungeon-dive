# Provisions for the Long Dark

The crate that arrived this cycle rattled with the wrong sort of cargo — no gems, no gold leaf, only fishhooks and ration tins, a cracked compass, and a single sodden diary whose final entry trailed off mid-word. The Keeper has learned to recognise the smell of such deliveries. This is the survival shelf, and it does not promise you will make it out.

Here are the games that strand you: on a fur-trapper's trail through the Colorado Rockies, in a raft adrift over a drowned world, in a dungeon your murdered companions never left, on a cursed island you have waited years to set foot upon. Some hand you a deck of story cards and dare you to choose badly; others hand you a fistful of dwindling resources and watch, patiently, to see which runs out first — your water, your rations, your resolve, or your luck. The wilderness here is not scenery. It is the antagonist, and it is winning.

A few of these expeditions the archive logged long ago — the ash-choked wastes of Fallen Land, the small furred survivors of the aftermath — and they belong on this shelf beside the newcomers, one continuous ledger of people trying not to die in inhospitable places. Read them in order and watch the genre itself learn to survive.

**Exhibit Catalogue** — ten expeditions into the unforgiving, earliest first:

- [Fallen Land: A Post-Apocalyptic Board Game - Take a Look](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1595) — factions scavenging the ashes of America, storytelling sprawl on a huge table.
- [Critter Crawl: A Discussion and Review of Aftermath](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1634) — small furred survivors picking through a post-apocalyptic world built for them.
- [Spire’s End - Take a Look](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1884) — a card-based descent where your allies are also your lives, and the D8 is cursed.
- [A Look at Rocky Mountain Man](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1880) — 1800s trappers, timed seasons, and a rulebook that reads John Muir before it draws the map.
- [A look at Unbroken - A solo survival, resource management dungeon crawl](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1883) — "if Tarantino made a dungeon crawl": left for dead, and out for revenge.
- [It’s finally time for Robinson Crusoe](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1882) — a years-awaited first landing on the cursed island, teased before the deep dive.
- [A Look at Spire's End: Hildegard](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1881) — soft artwork over a hard story, with a slingshot resolved in dice like Yahtzee.
- [Returning to the Apocalyptic Wasteland of Fallen Land](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1590) — a second trek through the ruined map, years on.
- [Broken Shores aka Godshard](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1879) — a drowned-world D100 sandbox where fresh water outvalues blood.
- [Posthuman Saga - Humans vs. Mutants in a struggle for survival](https://dungeondive.quest/t/1878) — a competitive race across the wasteland; skip the solo mode, play it two-handed.
