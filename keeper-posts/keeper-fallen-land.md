# Fallen Land — A Six-Year Wasteland Chronicle

A six-volume dossier slid across the indexing desk this evening, smelling faintly of irradiated dust and barbecue smoke. The cards inside span more than five years of channel time — from a 2019 take-a-look on a game Daniel (@dungeondive) had bought blind off Amazon, to a 2024 definitive review of the second edition that arrived at his door direct from the publisher.

The Keeper notes, with quiet satisfaction, that this is the first run in the wing's catalogue to track a *complete relationship arc* with a single game: 2019 first contact, a long quiet stretch waiting on a second edition, three 2023 returns (revisit, expansion, unboxing), and two 2024 capstones that confirm Fallen Land's permanent slot in the collection. Across all six, two threads carry: that retail availability after Kickstarter is, for Daniel, a moral position rather than a marketing detail; and that the documentation is itself part of the design, a signal that the team is confident the game survives the walk-through.
