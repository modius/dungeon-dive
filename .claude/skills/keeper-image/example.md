# Worked example — D100 Dungeon

Reference set illustrating the `keeper-image` house style. **Not a stored artefact** — the skill does not persist new prompt sets to disk; this file exists only to anchor the tone, structure, and conventions for future runs.

Subject: two principal characters from the D100 Dungeon series — **Hindar the Warrior of Ever Vamp** (the World Builder protagonist across four sessions) and **Baranof the Unfortunate** (the doomed dwarf rogue of session one). Five variants below — four for Hindar, one for Baranof. All in the cel-shaded cartoon house style. The Keeper (a mystical owl, archivist of the void between the shelves) is present in every composition — in witness register in the first four variants, and in archivist register in the final paper-life variant.

## 1. Hindar — heroic battlefield wide-shot

```
A weathered human warrior, mid-30s, broad-shouldered, standing on the windswept Grasslands of Horror — a vast battlefield-turned-graveyard at dusk with broken cairns and rusted weapons half-buried across the wide plain. He wields a notched two-handed bastard sword point-down in the earth, both hands resting on the pommel. Chainmail coif framing a bearded, stern face; dark leather cloak heavy with road-dust; scale-mail leggings; polished plate-mail girdle; three glass healing-potion vials clipped to a worn belt; a heavy bear-tooth necklace at his throat. A small wise-looking owl perched on the cross-guard of the sword, watching the viewer. Low blood-orange sun, distant murder of crows, the city of Ever Vamp a tiny silhouette on the horizon. Modern cel-shaded cartoon illustration, bold clean linework, flat saturated colour with subtle gradient shading, expressive character design --ar 16:9 --v 8
```

## 2. Hindar — mid-combat in the snake-pit trap

```
A human warrior plunged into a deep stone pit lit only by his fallen lantern, swinging a two-handed bastard sword in a wide arc against a coiling monstrous serpent, scales gleaming, fangs bared. Chainmail coif, leather cloak streaming, scale-mail leggings braced against the rubble floor, glass potion vials swinging from his belt. A small owl peering down anxiously from the lip of the pit far above on the left side of the frame, silhouetted against torchlight. Falling stones, dust caught in horizontal shafts of light cutting across the wide cavern. Dynamic cel-shaded cartoon illustration, bold linework, dramatic motion lines, high-contrast flat colour, expressive action pose, wide cinematic framing --ar 16:9 --v 8
```

## 3. Hindar — Ever Vamp tavern aftermath (robbed)

```
A battered human warrior slumped on a wooden bench outside a torch-lit fantasy tavern at night, holding the back of his head, one eye blackened, looking ruefully at an empty belt slot where a pouch used to be. Cloak askew, chainmail coif loose, healing potions missing from the belt, bastard sword leaning against the wall beside him. A small owl perched on the tavern signpost above him on the right of the frame, looking down with patient disapproval. Wet cobblestone street stretching off into the night, a few cartoon patrons stepping over him into the inn on the left, warm tavern light spilling across the stones. Melancholy cel-shaded cartoon illustration, bold linework, flat colour with warm rim light, wide street-level framing --ar 16:9 --v 8
```

## 4. Baranof the Unfortunate — the doomed rookie

```
A young, scrappy dwarf rogue with a wildly bewildered expression, mid-fall into a stone pit-trap, arms flailing, short bow swinging on its strap. Leather cap askew, leather gloves, leather tassit, half-empty quiver. To the right of the frame in the dim background a leering goatman priest with dark-magic glow and giant spiderwebs strung across a doorway; to the left, a second pit trap visible in cross-section. A small owl perched on a rafter overhead with one wing covering its eyes, refusing to watch. Crumbling dungeon corridor in cross-section, torchlight, dust motes. Comedic cel-shaded cartoon illustration, bold linework, exaggerated comic motion, expressive cartoon faces, the moment just before things go very badly, wide side-scroller composition --ar 16:9 --v 8
```

## 5. Paper-life framing — Hindar on the hex map (Keeper in archivist register)

```
A small human warrior figure, sword on shoulder, standing heroically at the centre of a vast hand-drawn hex-grid map laid out across an oak desk that fills the wide frame. Around him, in faded pencil and ink: the Grasslands of Horror, the city of Ever Vamp marked as a small square, a meandering river and road, a Forest of Elves to the east, the Tundra of Coldness to the north, the Hills of Doom to the south. At the right edge of the desk, perched directly on a large open ledger, a mystical wise-looking owl — The Keeper — holding a quill in one talon, a pair of tiny round spectacles balanced on his beak, a few index cards drifting around him on faint currents of air, vast dark library shelves receding into mist behind. D10 and percentile dice scattered across the desk, a graphite pencil, an eraser. Top-down three-quarter view, warm desk-lamp light from above. Cel-shaded cartoon illustration, bold clean linework, flat colour, tabletop-RPG storybook charm, wide cinematic desk-top composition --ar 16:9 --v 8
```
